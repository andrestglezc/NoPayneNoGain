Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

No Payne No Gain ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website timpaynefans.vercel.app and the No Payne No Gain mobile application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Usage data. We collect anonymous interaction data — tap counts, chant generations, and mission completions — to power the global tap counter and improve the experience. This data is not linked to any individual.
  • Device data. Standard server logs may capture your IP address, browser type, and referring URL when you visit the website. This data is used for security and performance monitoring and is not sold or shared.
  • Locally stored data. The mobile app stores your streak, badges, and Payne Points locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information. We do not require account registration. We do not use advertising networks or sell data to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

The website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts. Vercel, our hosting provider, may collect aggregate traffic statistics in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The Service links to Tim Payne's Instagram profile (instagram.com). When you follow that link, Instagram's own privacy policy applies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or practices of third-party sites.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
